Sunday School Summer Program for 2022

Sunday School Summer Program starts on 14th July, 2022. 

Currently offering two (2) classes and three (3) levels. Level 2 (7-8 years old), Level 3 (9-12 years old) and teens (13 & up). The first period from 6:30 to 7:20 pm and second period from 7:30 to 8:20 pm. The classes will run for five (5) weeks. Costs $70/student/program.
