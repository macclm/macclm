About Us

OUR VISION

Our vision is to establish a Center of Excellence, in which young generation can pursue the core values of Islam (Peace) Ehsan (Unity) and Iman (Faith) which make them a good American citizens and excellent role model for the world.


OUR MISSION

Muslim American Community Center of Lexington, Massachusetts (MACCLM) is a non-profit  registered religious and educational organization. Our religious activities are guided by the principles of the Holy Quran and Sunnah of our beloved Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). Activties such as teaching STEAM (Science, Technology, Engneering, Arts and Mathematics), arranging robotics courses to elementary, middle and high school students are also our main focus. In addition, our other social activities also include children's after school activities, meetups for stay-home parents and seniors, health advice, sports and celebration of national holidays. Moreover, our services are also open to other faiths.
