2022 Summer Robotics Course at MACCLM

Dear Sisters and Brothers,

With the grace of Allah we are continuing with our Robotics course series this summer of 2022. The program will start on Wednesday July 20TH and end on August 22ND. The program will run on Mondays & Wednesdays for 5 weeks.

Some of the concepts covered will be STEAM fundamentals, electric machines, computer science, and their applications in robotics. They will get very high applied mathematics skills that would support them in fundamental mathematics they learn in their school.

Days and timings: Monday & Wednesday: Session 1 from 9:00-10:00am, Session 2 from 10:00-11:00am, Session 3 from 11:00am-12:00pm, Session 4 from 12:00-1:00pm, Session 5 from 1:00-2:00pm, Session 6 from 2:00-3:00pm, Session 7 from 3:00-4:00pm, Session 8 from 4:00-5:00pm and Session 9 from 5:00-6:00pm.

Age Range: 4yrs old and above

Mode: in-person one-to-two or one-to-three twice a week sessions using both theoretical and practical approaches, customized sessions for each student

Fees: $200 paid in advance.

Teacher: Dr. Atilla Akil

Open house on Monday July 18TH from 6:00-8:00pm. Please stop by to drop off the registration form and make payment. Teachers will be present to answer any questions.

Looking forward to seeing you.

Sincerely,

MACCLM Team

2022 Summer Coding Course at MACCLM

Dear Sisters and Brothers,

With the grace of Allah we are offering a coding course for our youngsters this summer. The program will start on Wednesday July 20TH and end on August 22ND.

The program will run on Mondays & Wednesdays for 5 weeks. This is an introductory course designed to introduce students to the field of coding. Students will learn fundamentals of coding, Java programming and develop coding skills.

Days and timings: Monday & Wednesday: Session 1 from 4:00-5:00pm, Session 2 from 5:00-6:00pm, Session 3 from 6:00-7:00pm and Session 4 from 7:00-8:00pm.

Age Range: 12yrs old and above

Mode: in-person one hour session twice a week, 3-4 students per session, 10 sessions each student, students to bring laptops for their session

Fees: $100 paid in advance.

Teacher: Keyan Shahidullah (Senior in Lexington High School)

Open house on Monday July 18TH from 6:00-8:00pm. Please stop by to drop off the registration form and make payment. Teachers will be present to answer any questions.

Looking forward to seeing you.

Sincerely,

MACCLM Team
